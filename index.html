Recipe Details


Ingredients:

- 1 box (2 count) refrigerated pie crusts
- 6-7 medium apples (e.g., Granny Smith, Honeycrisp), peeled, cored, sliced
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg (optional)
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)

Recipe:

1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. Unroll one pie crust and gently place it into a 9-inch pie plate.
3. In a large bowl, combine sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice (if using). Toss until apples are evenly coated.
4. Pour the apple mixture into the pie crust-lined plate. Dot the top with small pieces of butter.
5. Unroll the second pie crust and place it over the apple filling. Trim and crimp the edges to seal.
6. Cut several small slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
7. Bake for 15 minutes at 425°F (220°C).
8. Reduce oven temperature to 375°F (190°)
